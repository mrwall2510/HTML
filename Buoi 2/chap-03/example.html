Scrambled eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bow
  3. Once butter hs mel ted add cream and eggs of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
